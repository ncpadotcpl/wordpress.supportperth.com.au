# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Perth small-business owners who run their business on a WordPress site. Two arrival states: the emergency visitor (site down, white screen, hacked, locked out of wp-admin — stressed, searching on their phone, needs a human today) and the planning visitor (wants a new build, a migration, or ongoing maintenance and is comparing local options). Neither is technical; both are allergic to agency jargon and ticket queues. The surface speaks directly to the person who owns the problem, not an IT department.

## Product Purpose

A single-page landing site at https://wordpress.supportperth.com.au for Support Perth's WordPress-specific services: new builds and rebuilds, maintenance and updates, emergency fixes, hosting and migrations, security hardening and malware cleanup, speed / Core Web Vitals, WooCommerce support, and content & SEO upkeep. Success is a qualified lead through the contact modal (email or phone) or a direct phone call — no pricing on the page, everything drives to contact.

## Positioning

The Perth senior engineer who fixes your WordPress problem the same day — no ticket queue, no lock-in.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: the contact modal (any #contact link site-wide) — email or phone, either one alone is enough.
- Secondary CTA: call 1300 769 337 directly (tel: links in hero, emergency band, final CTA, footer).
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: Quality WordPress support in Perth — same-day, from a real person.
- Belief ladder: (1) They handle my exact problem — symptom chips in the visitor's own words, eight named services. (2) They're real, local and proven — 5.0 Google rating across 25 reviews, named testimonials. (3) Reaching out is zero-risk — email or phone, same-day reply, no lock-in, no pricing pressure.
- Proof on hand: 5.0 Google rating across 25 reviews; the Gavin K. pull-quote ("Aaron is a pleasure to deal with … Helped us out enormously."). Client logos (JoDoKo, Be Slavery Free, Safestar Supports, Arena Joondalup Physiotherapy) exist on the main site as testimonial-only marks — never hero or decorative imagery.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, reliable, plain-spoken. A senior engineer speaking directly to Perth small-business owners: short sentences, concrete promises (same-day response, no lock-in, month-to-month), real numbers over adjectives, a light touch of personality. Preferred vocabulary: no lock-in, same-day response, senior engineer, month-to-month, free no-obligation chat, Perth. Avoid: enterprise buzzwords (synergy, leverage, solutions), pushy sales language, vague promises without numbers.

## Anti-references

Enterprise MSP sites full of buzzwords (synergy, solutions, leverage). Stock photos of server rooms, headsets or call centres. Pushy sales pages with fake urgency or vague promises without numbers. Generic AI-template landing pages. Client logos used as hero or brand imagery.

## Design Principles

1. Speak the visitor's panic language — the emergency band names symptoms exactly as the visitor saw them ("White screen of death", the literal database-connection error), not service categories.
2. Proof over promises — every claim carries a real number or a named source; missing values get honest placeholders, never invented stats.
3. Zero-risk contact — the modal asks for email or phone (either suffices), validates only on submit, and never pressures with pricing or urgency.
4. Technology as a calm connected mesh — the ocean-blue node-network is the identity imagery; no stock photography, ever.
5. Same-day means visibly alive — availability dot, same-day pills, pulsing signals; responsiveness is shown, not just claimed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA is the target (the standard the site's audits already measure against). All motion honours prefers-reduced-motion; autoplaying hero video requires a reduced-motion pause path; modal errors must be announced (role="alert"); touch targets ≥44px; contrast minimums 4.5:1 body / 3:1 large text verified on every band, including the dark ink and ocean-blue surfaces.
