Fair questions about WordPress support in Perth

Can you fix a WordPress site someone else built?

Yes — most of our work is on sites we didn't build. We start with a plain-English diagnosis of what's there, then fix what's broken without forcing a rebuild you don't need.

My site has been hacked. What happens first?

We isolate the site so it can't do more damage, clean the infection, patch the entry point, request removal from Google's blocklists, and hand you a short report on how it got in and what we hardened.

Will moving hosts break my email or my Google rankings?

No. We stage the migration on the new host first, map every URL one-to-one, and only switch DNS once everything is verified — so email keeps flowing and rankings carry over.

Do you work with page builders and WooCommerce?

Yes — Elementor, Divi, the block editor, and WooCommerce stores. For stores we prioritise checkout, payments and shipping issues, because downtime there costs you directly.

Am I locked into a contract?

No. Ongoing care is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. One-off fixes are exactly that — one-off.

How fast do you respond?

Same day for standard requests. If your site is down or hacked, it jumps the queue.
